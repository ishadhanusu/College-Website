ABOUT US

Welcome to University College of Engineering Villupuram University College of Engineering Villupuram is one among the 13 Constituent Colleges in Tamil Nadu under Anna University, Chennai and is a Co-educational College for Graduate and Post Graduate Students. The Institution is approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) and it is one among the outstanding College in Villupuram District. The following are the under-graduate and post-graduate Courses offered by the Institution with an intake of 60 students in each discipline.

B.E. - Mechanical Engineering (English Medium)
B.E. - Mechanical Engineering (Tamil Medium)
B.E. - Electronics and Communication Engineering
B.E. - Computer Science Engineering
B.Tech. - Information Technology
M.E.- Internal Combustion Engineering ( intake of 25 students)

TRINING & PLACEMENT CELL


The Training & Placement Cell (TPC) of University College of Engineering Villupuram (UCEV) was established during the academic year 2010. The TPC performs all placement related activities under the guidance/support of Dean, UCEV and Centre for University - Industry Collaboration (CUIC), Anna University Chennai. The main focus of TPC is to produce professionals with sound technical skills and desires to make them compete in global arena. The TPC ensures a conducive atmosphere to create good relationship between institute and corporate. The TPC provides necessary infrastructure and hospitality to the guests for conducting training programmes and placement drives.

VISION


The TPC of UCEV is focused to create expertise in the field of engineering and technology. The TCP pays more attention to mould the students with good technical competence and managerial skills to meet the industry expectations. The TPC is focused to create strong bridge between UCEV graduates and Industrial corporate.
