DEPARTMENTS

  • Electronics and Communication Engineering

    • The Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE) was started in the year 2008 with the vision of developing the Department as a Centre of Excellence in R & D in the field of Electronics and Communication Engineering. The Department aims at making the students blossom into meritorious and self disciplined Engineers with dedication and hardwork. Mission of the Department is to develop innovative and simple instructional approach.

  • Information Technology

    • The Department of Information Technology was established in the year 2008. The primary objective of the department is to provide an in-depth knowledge in the field of Information Technology. The department endeavors to produce confident professionals tuned to real time working environment.

  • Computer Science Engineering

    • Since its inception in 2008, the Department of Computer Science and Engineering has been continuously making progress in teaching and research activities. The Department offers the course by following the syllabus and curriculum framed by Anna University.
